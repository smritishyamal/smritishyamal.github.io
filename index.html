Smriti Shyamal is currently doing Ph.D. at McMaster University, Canada, where he is supervised by Dr. Christoper L.E. Swartz. He received B.Tech degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, India in 2013 and subsequently joined McMaster University. His current research focus is on data science, machine learning, optimization and control applications.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning

  • Deep learning

  • Reinforcement learning

  • Optimization algorithms

  • Large scale applications
